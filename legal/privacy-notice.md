## Section 1 - Introduction

a. This Privacy Notice ("Notice") explains which personal data is collected when you visit our website [https://tins8.at](https://tins8.at) ("Website") and how this data is processed by tins8 GmbH as the data controller ("tins8" or "we").

c. We process your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable national data protection laws.

## Section 2 - What Personal Data We Collect and How We Use It

a. When you use our Website, we may process personal data that you voluntarily provide to us (for example through order or contact forms).

b. You can also visit our Website without actively providing information. In that case, we collect certain data your browser transmits to our website server (log files), as well as data collected via cookies and similar technologies.

c. The following explanations describe how we may collect personal data and for which lawful purposes we may use it.

d. Personal data you actively disclose to us:

1. When you actively communicate with us via our Website, we process personal data voluntarily provided by you. This includes, in particular, the following services:

   a. Web shop: If you purchase one of our products via the Website, we ask for mandatory information (for example: name, company, VAT number, address, email, order details, and payment data such as credit card or invoice details). Unless otherwise communicated, we process these data solely for processing your order, facilitating payment, and delivering the product. Legal basis: Art 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract).

   b. Email contacts and chatbots: If you contact us via email or chatbot, we process data you provide (for example: name, email address, correspondence data) to create a contact record, answer questions, fulfill requests, or otherwise communicate with you. Legal basis: Art 6(1)(b) and Art 6(1)(f) GDPR (pre-contractual/contractual duties and legitimate interest in customer relationship management). The chatbot is hosted by HubSpot, Inc. as data processor on our behalf.

   c. Marketing emails: If you subscribe to our marketing email list via the Website, or otherwise provide your business contact details, we may send publications, event invitations, and/or news that may be of interest. Legal basis: Art 6(1)(b) and Art 6(1)(f) GDPR.

2. Some requested fields are mandatory. You are not required to provide this data; however, without it we may not be able to process your request or provide services.

e. Log files:

1. If you visit our Website without actively providing information, we still collect certain technical data sent by your browser (log files).

2. Log files may contain: (i) date/time of retrieval, (ii) browser type/version/settings, (iii) operating system and internet service provider, (iv) requested pages/files, (v) referring website, and (vi) IP address.

3. Processing these log files is necessary to maintain functionality, stability, and security of our Website. We may also process them for forensic investigations in case of security incidents and for traffic statistics.

4. Legal basis: Art 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest).

f. Cookies:

1. This Website uses cookies. Cookies are small text files placed on your device during browsing that store certain information.

2. We use two categories of cookies:

   A. Necessary cookies: Required for proper operation (authentication status, checkout/session state, payment process support, cookie preference handling, chatbot/session preferences). Legal basis: Art 6(1)(f) GDPR.

   B. Optional cookies: Used to improve user experience, analyze behavior, and optimize marketing. Optional cookies may be placed by analytics and third-party tooling (for example HubSpot). Optional cookies are only used based on your consent. Legal basis: Art 6(1)(a) GDPR.

g. Web analytics (Matomo):

1. If you consent to optional cookies, this Website may use the open-source analytics software Matomo (InnoCraft Ltd., 150 Willis St, 6011 Wellington, New Zealand).

2. Information generated by cookies about use of the Website (including IP metadata and accessed URLs) may be processed for analytics. IP addresses can be anonymized (for example via truncation).

3. Analytics is used to evaluate website usage, produce reports, and support service improvements.

4. Legal basis: Art 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent).

h. How to control and manage cookies:

1. By accepting optional cookies in the Website cookie banner, you consent to optional cookie use.

2. By refusing optional cookies in the cookie banner, optional cookies are not used.

3. You can withdraw consent at any time with future effect by contacting [hello@tins8.at](mailto:hello@tins8.at) and/or via the cookie controls available on the Website.

4. You may also manage cookies via browser settings.

   - Firefox: [support.mozilla.org](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer?redirectlocale=en-US&redirectslug=Cookies)
   - Chrome: [support.google.com](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en)
   - Safari: [support.apple.com](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)

5. Please note that disabling cookies may reduce functionality.
